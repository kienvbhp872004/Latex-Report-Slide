


TONG HOP LY THUYET
Image Decomposition trong bai toan Image Fusion
SOTA 2022 - 2025


Bao gom: Multi-scale Transform | Feature Decomp. | Diffusion | Retinex | INN
 
1. Gioi thieu tong quan
Image Fusion la bai toan ket hop thong tin tu nhieu anh nguon khac modality (infrared + visible, MRI + CT, multifocus...) nham tao ra mot anh duy nhat chua day du thong tin bo sung. Ung dung tieu bieu: giam sat an ninh, chan doan y te, xe tu lai, vien tham, robotics.

Image Decomposition la thanh phan then chot trong hau het pipeline fusion SOTA. Y tuong cot loi: phan tach anh nguon thanh cac thanh phan doc lap de ap dung chien luoc fusion toi uu cho tung thanh phan truoc khi tong hop lai anh ket qua.

Ghi chu: Tai lieu nay tong hop ly thuyet nen tang, phan tich toan hoc va so sanh cac phuong phap decomposition noi bat tu 2022 den dau 2025.

1.1. Tai sao can Decomposition?
Trong fusion, hai loai thong tin can xu ly khac nhau:
•	Base / Low-frequency: thong tin cuong do nen, cau truc toan cuc (illumination, large-scale structure). Yeu cau tich hop nhat quan, tranh artifact.
•	Detail / High-frequency: canh, ket cau, gradient cuc bo, nhieu. Yeu cau bao toan va lam sac net thong tin chi tiet tung modality.

Neu ap dung fusion rule dong nhat cho toan bo anh, thuong dan den: mo canh, mat chi tiet texture, hoac sai lech mau sac. Decomposition cho phep thiet ke fusion rule rieng biet va phu hop.
 
2. Nhom 1: Multi-scale Transform Decomposition
Nhom phuong phap truyen thong voi nen tang toan hoc chat che, su dung cac bien doi toan hoc de phan tach anh thanh cac dai tan so hoac ti le (scale) khac nhau.

2.1. Discrete Wavelet Transform (DWT)
2.1.1. Nen tang ly thuyet
DWT phan tach tin hieu 2D bang cach ap dung bo loc low-pass h[n] va high-pass g[n] theo ca hai chieu (hang va cot), sau do downsample 2x.

Cho anh I(x,y), mot cap DWT tao ra 4 sub-band:
•	LL: xap xi (approximation) – thong tin low-frequency, global structure
•	LH: chi tiet nam ngang – canh nam ngang
•	HL: chi tiet doc – canh doc
•	HH: chi tiet cheo – goc, ket cau min

Phuong trinh bien doi thuan (Forward DWT) cap 1:
cLL(m,n) = Sum_k Sum_l  h[k] h[l] . I(2m-k, 2n-l)
cLH(m,n) = Sum_k Sum_l  g[k] h[l] . I(2m-k, 2n-l)

Bien doi nguoc (IDWT) cho phep tai tao anh ma khong mat thong tin (perfect reconstruction) khi bo loc thoa man dieu kien biorthogonal.

2.1.2. Quy trinh Fusion voi DWT
•	Buoc 1: DWT cho tung anh nguon I1, I2 → {LL1, LH1, HL1, HH1} va {LL2, LH2, HL2, HH2}
•	Buoc 2: Fusion sub-band LL (low-freq): weighted average hoac max-intensity rule
•	Buoc 3: Fusion sub-band LH/HL/HH (high-freq): max-absolute rule hoac activity-measure-based rule:
  F_HH(m,n) = argmax_{k in {1,2}}  |c_HH^k(m,n)|
•	Buoc 4: IDWT tren cac sub-band da fusion → anh ket qua

2.1.3. Bien the SOTA: WaveFusionNet (2024)
WaveFusionNet tich hop DWT vao encoder-decoder: cac lop DWT thay the max-pooling, giam mat thong tin spatial. Feature wavelet duoc feed vao attention module de tu dong hoc fusion weight thay vi dung hand-crafted rule.

Ghi chu: Uu diem DWT: perfect reconstruction, nen tang ly thuyet chat. Nhuoc diem: shift-variant, it huong (3 huong: H, V, D).

2.2. Non-subsampled Shearlet Transform (NSST)
2.2.1. Dong luc va ly thuyet
DWT chi co 3 huong. NSST ket hop Non-subsampled Contourlet Transform (NSCT) va Shearlet de dat phan giai da huong, da ti le ma khong aliasing (nho khong downsample).

Shearlet psi duoc dinh nghia qua: psi_{j,l,k}(x) = a^{3j/4} psi(S_l A_a^j x - k)
A_a: ma tran scale anisotropic | S_l: ma tran shear | k: vector dich chuyen

NSST khong downsample → sub-band cung kich thuoc voi anh goc → de thiet ke fusion rule tai tung vi tri pixel.

Ghi chu: Ung dung: NSST dac biet hieu qua trong Medical Image Fusion (MRI-CT, PET-MRI) nho kha nang bat canh da huong.

2.3. Gaussian / Laplacian Pyramid
2.3.1. Cau truc Pyramid
Gaussian Pyramid tao chuoi anh mo dan: G_0 ⊃ G_1 ⊃ ... ⊃ G_N. Moi cap thu nho bang Gaussian kernel roi downsample 2x:
G_{l+1}(i,j) = Sum_{m,n} w(m,n) . G_l(2i+m, 2j+n)

Laplacian Pyramid la su khac biet giua cac cap Gaussian:
L_l = G_l - EXPAND(G_{l+1})
EXPAND: upsample + blur. Moi L_l chua thong tin detail tai ti le tuong ung. G_N (cap thap nhat) la residual base image.

2.3.2. Octave Gaussian Pyramid (2024)
Chia pyramid thanh octave (cac level cung kich thuoc) va interval (level trong moi octave), cho phep thu nhieu cap base-detail hon. Dung guided filter de tang sharpness o buoc upsample, giam halo artifact.

2.4. Guided Filter & Edge-Preserving Decomposition
2.4.1. Guided Filter
Guided Filter (He et al., 2013) dua tren mo hinh tuyen tinh cuc bo:
q_i = a_k . p_i + b_k,  voi i thuoc omega_k
trong do p_i la anh input, q_i la output, omega_k la cua so cuc bo tai k. He so (a_k, b_k) tinh bang cach toi thieu hoa:
E(a_k, b_k) = Sum_{i in omega_k} [(a_k.G_i + b_k - p_i)^2 + epsilon.a_k^2]
Guided image G (thuong la anh goc hoac anh canh) giup bo loc bao toan canh manh.

2.4.2. MLGEPF (2024)
Multilevel Guided Edge-Preserving Filter xep chong nhieu cap guided filter tao multi-level decomposition nhanh O(n) ma khong dung DFT. Dac biet phu hop cho anh y te (PET-MRI, CT-MRI) vi bao toan structure.

2.5. Wavelet + Mamba: WMambaFuse (2025)
WMambaFuse ket hop hai module song song: WMFM (Wavelet-domain Mamba Fusion Module) xu ly thong tin frequency, va SAFM (Spatial Attention Fusion Module) xu ly khong gian. Swin Transformer encoder trich xuat feature da ti le. Mamba (SSM) duoc dung thay vi self-attention truyen thong O(n^2) de xu ly chuan token dai hieu qua hon (O(n)).

Ghi chu: Xu huong 2024-2025: thay Transformer bang Mamba/SSM trong frequency domain de giam chi phi tinh toan tren anh high-resolution.
 
3. Nhom 2: Feature Decomposition (Deep Learning)
Nhom nay hoc cach phan tach truc tiep tu du lieu thay vi dung transform co dinh. Mang neural tu tim ra bieu dien toi uu cho fusion task.

3.1. Base-Detail Decomposition: CDDFuse (CVPR 2023)
3.1.1. Kien truc
CDDFuse (Correlation-Driven Dual-Branch Fusion) la mot trong nhung mo hinh SOTA quan trong nhat 2023. Kien truc gom:
•	Lite Transformer (LT) Block: xu ly low-frequency global feature (base). Transformer hoc moi quan he tam xa, phu hop de nam bat cau truc toan cuc.
•	Invertible Neural Network (INN) Block: xu ly high-frequency local detail. INN dam bao thong tin detail khong bi mat nho tinh kha nghich.
•	Correlation-Driven Loss: dieu khien qua trinh hoc decomposition.

3.1.2. Correlation-Driven Loss – Dong gop ly thuyet cot loi
(a) Correlation Loss cho Base Features – bat buoc base feature hai modality phai tuong quan cao (chia se thong tin cau truc chung):
L_base = 1 - rho(F_base^{IR}, F_base^{VIS})
trong do rho la Pearson correlation coefficient: rho(X,Y) = Cov(X,Y) / (sigma_X . sigma_Y)

(b) Decorrelation Loss cho Detail Features – bat buoc detail feature hai modality phai it tuong quan (moi modality co chi tiet rieng biet):
L_detail = |rho(F_detail^{IR}, F_detail^{VIS})|

Tong loss:
L_total = lambda1.L_base + lambda2.L_detail + lambda3.L_recon + lambda4.L_perception

Ghi chu: Y tuong: base features correlate cao → fuse bang weighted avg. Detail features decorrelate → fuse bang max/concat de giu tinh rieng biet cua tung modality.

3.1.3. Qua trinh Fusion
•	Base fusion: F_base^F = (F_base^{IR} + F_base^{VIS}) / 2
•	Detail fusion: F_detail^F = alpha . F_detail^{IR} + (1-alpha) . F_detail^{VIS} voi alpha hoc tu attention module
•	Reconstruction: IDWT/decoder tu (F_base^F, F_detail^F) → fused image

3.2. Modality-Specific / Shared Decomposition
3.2.1. Ly thuyet Disentanglement
Muc tieu hoc bieu dien factorized: tach shared content (cau truc giai phau, hinh dang vat the) va modality-specific content (thermal radiation, texture rieng).

Cho encoder E_s (shared) va E_m (modality-specific), decoder D, anh nguon I^A, I^B:
•	z_s^A = E_s(I^A), z_s^B = E_s(I^B) → phai giong nhau (shared)
•	z_m^A = E_m(I^A), z_m^B = E_m(I^B) → phai khac nhau (modality-specific)
•	I^A = D(z_s^A, z_m^A) (reconstruction)

Loss disentanglement thuong dung:
•	Cross-reconstruction: I^{A→B} = D(z_s^A, z_m^B) phai giong I^B
•	MK-MMD (Multi-Kernel Maximum Mean Discrepancy): do distance giua distributions trong RKHS:
  MMD^2(X,Y) = ||mu_X - mu_Y||^2_H

3.2.2. DAF-Net (2024)
DAF-Net dung Multi-Kernel MMD trong base encoder de align latent space IR va VIS. INN-based detail encoder tach rieng texture tung modality. Cross-attention giua shared va specific features truoc khi fusion.

3.3. Semantic Layer Decomposition: PSFusion (2023)
Phan tach theo semantic levels dua tren do sau trong mang backbone (ResNet). Layer nong (early) chua low-level feature (canh, mau sac); layer sau (late) chua high-level semantic feature (object, class).
F_l = E_l(I)  voi l = 1..L (layer index)
F^{fused} = Sum_l w_l . Fusion(F_l^{IR}, F_l^{VIS})

PSFusion khai thac ResNet backbone trich xuat feature tai nhieu semantic level, thiet ke Progressive Semantic Fusion Module tu tho den tinh.

3.4. Transformer-CNN Hybrid (DAF-Net, 2024)
Mo hinh hybrid dung Transformer va CNN song song:
•	Transformer branch: self-attention tren patch → global feature → base component
•	CNN branch: conv layers → local feature → detail component
•	Cross-branch fusion: cross-attention giua hai nhanh de bo sung thong tin
 
4. Nhom 3: Generative / Diffusion-based Decomposition
Nhom nay khong phan tach anh theo cach truyen thong ma formulate fusion nhu bai toan conditional generation hoac Bayesian inference trong khong gian latent cua mo hinh sinh.

4.1. Nen tang: Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models (DDPM)
4.1.1. Ly thuyet DDPM (Ho et al., 2020)
Forward process (diffusion): them Gaussian noise vao du lieu x0 qua T buoc:
q(x_t | x_{t-1}) = N(x_t;  sqrt(1-beta_t).x_{t-1},  beta_t.I)
Closed form: q(x_t|x_0) = N(x_t;  sqrt(alpha_bar_t).x_0,  (1-alpha_bar_t).I)
voi: alpha_bar_t = Product_{s=1}^{t} (1 - beta_s)

Reverse process (denoising): hoc loai noise de tai tao anh tu noise:
p_theta(x_{t-1}|x_t) = N(x_{t-1};  mu_theta(x_t,t),  Sigma_theta(x_t,t))
Mang noise predictor eps_theta train voi loss:
L_DDPM = E_{x0, eps, t} [|| eps - eps_theta(sqrt(alpha_bar_t).x0 + sqrt(1-alpha_bar_t).eps, t) ||^2]

4.1.2. DDFM – Fusion nhu Bayesian Posterior Sampling (ICCV 2023 Oral)
DDFM formulate fusion nhu tinh posterior p(x_F | I_IR, I_VIS) va phan ra bang EM algorithm:
p(x_F | I_IR, I_VIS)  ∝  p(x_F) . p(I_IR, I_VIS | x_F)

•	Subproblem 1 – Unconditional score: ∇_{x_t} log p_theta(x_t) → tinh bang score function cua pre-trained DDPM (khong can re-train)
•	Subproblem 2 – Likelihood score: ∇_{x_t} log p(I_IR, I_VIS | x_t) → mo hinh hoa bang hierarchical Bayesian:
  log p(I | x_t) = Sum_i log p(I_i | z_i) + log p(z_i | x_t)
Trong do z_i la latent variable cho modality i, toi uu bang EM: E-step (estimate z_i), M-step (update gradient).

Ghi chu: Uu diem: DDFM khong can training data fusion (zero-shot); chi can pre-trained diffusion model. Nhuoc diem: inference cham (nhieu denoising steps T).

4.2. Frequency-domain Diffusion: Dif-Fusion (2023)
Dif-Fusion phan tach anh theo frequency domain truoc khi ap dung diffusion: low-frequency fusion bang weighted average; high-frequency enhance bang guided diffusion process. Bao toan color fidelity va tang sharpness dong thoi.

4.3. VLM-Guided Fusion (ICML 2024)
Su dung Vision-Language Model (CLIP, BLIP-2) de huong dan decomposition va fusion. Text prompt (vi du: 'preserve pedestrian thermal signature') duoc encode thanh text embedding, cross-attend voi image feature de dieu khien fusion weight. Decomposition tro nen task-adaptive.
 
5. Nhom 4: Physical-Prior Decomposition

5.1. Retinex Decomposition
5.1.1. Ly thuyet Retinex (Land & McCann, 1971)
Mo hinh hoa anh quan sat I nhu tich cua hai thanh phan:
I(x,y) = L(x,y) . R(x,y)
trong do L(x,y) la illumination map (anh sang nen, bien doi cham) va R(x,y) la reflectance map (noi dung thuc, mau sac va canh).

Trong log domain: log I = log L + log R
Illumination L uoc luong bang Total Variation minimization:
L* = argmin_L { ||log I - log L||^2 + lambda . TV(log L) }

5.1.2. Ung dung trong Low-light Image Fusion
IR: thong tin thermal reliable, cao tuong phan. VIS: thong tin mau sac. Sau Retinex decomposition:
•	Lay illumination L tu IR (IR co do tuong phan cao va nhat quan)
•	Lay reflectance R tu VIS (VIS co thong tin mau sac)
•	Tai tao: I_F = L_IR . R_VIS (enhanced, colorful, high-contrast)

5.2. Hilbert Vibration Decomposition (HVD, 2024)
5.2.1. Ly thuyet HVD
HVD la phuong phap adaptive decomposition khong can basis function co dinh. Tin hieu f(t) bieu dien la tong cac thanh phan dao dong:
f(t) = Sum_k A_k(t) . cos(phi_k(t)) + r(t)
trong do A_k(t) la instantaneous amplitude va phi_k(t) la instantaneous phase. Phep bien doi Hilbert tinh analytical signal:
f_a(t) = f(t) + i.H{f(t)} = A(t).exp(i.phi(t))

Khac voi EMD (Empirical Mode Decomposition), HVD khong dung sifting process nen it mode mixing. HVD decompose anh 2D thanh spatial frequency components non-stationary (bien doi theo vi tri).

Ghi chu: Uu diem: khong can xac dinh cut-off frequency truoc, thich nghi voi local signal structure. Phu hop cho anh co texture phuc tap.
 
6. Nhom 5: Invertible Neural Network (INN) / Flow-based
Su dung tinh kha nghich (invertibility) nhu rang buoc kien truc, dam bao khong co thong tin nao bi mat. Quan trong trong y te (interpretability, reconstruction).

6.1. Nen tang toan hoc cua INN
6.1.1. Affine Coupling Layer (RealNVP, Dinh et al. 2017)
Cho input x = (x1, x2):
y1 = x1
y2 = x2 * exp(s(x1)) + t(x1)
Trong do s(.) va t(.) la cac mang neural tuy y (khong can kha nghich). Bien doi nguoc:
x1 = y1
x2 = (y2 - t(y1)) * exp(-s(y1))

Jacobian la triangular → determinant tinh de dang: log|det J| = Sum s(x1)

6.1.2. Change of Variables Formula
Voi INN f: x → z, likelihood cua du lieu:
log p(x) = log p_Z(f(x)) + log |det(df/dx)|
Trong do p_Z la prior (Gaussian). Training maximize log-likelihood nay.

6.2. Haar Wavelet Downsampling trong INN
Thay vi strided convolution (mat thong tin), nhieu INN dung Haar wavelet downsampling de squeeze spatial dimensions vao channel dimension:
x thuoc R^{H×W×C}  →  z thuoc R^{H/2×W/2×4C}
Day la phep bien doi kha nghich, preserve thong tin qua tat ca layers.

6.3. FusionINN – End-to-end Invertible Fusion (2024)
6.3.1. Framework
FusionINN tich hop dong thoi image decomposition va fusion vao mot normalizing flow end-to-end:
(z_s, z_A, z_B) = f_INN(I^A, I^B)
Trong do z_s la shared latent, z_A, z_B la modality-specific latents. Fused image:
I^F = f_INN^{-1}(z_s, 0, 0)

Tai tao source images:
I^A_hat = f_INN^{-1}(z_s, z_A, 0)
Kha nang tai tao source images tu fused image la dac tinh quan trong trong ung dung y te.

6.3.2. Training Objective
•	Reconstruction loss: ||I^A - I^A_hat||_1 + ||I^B - I^B_hat||_1
•	Fusion quality: perceptual loss + gradient loss tren I^F
•	Disentanglement: KL divergence align z_s; khuyen khich z_A, z_B orthogonal

6.4. EMMA – Equivariant Multi-Modal Alignment (CVPR 2024)
EMMA su dung equivariant network de hoc phep bien doi giua modalities. Equivariance dam bao: neu anh bi transform (rotate, flip), feature map cung transform tuong ung → alignment tot hon giua IR va VIS khi co misalignment.

9. Xu huong nghien cuu 2024-2025

9.1. Ket hop da phuong phap (Hybrid)
Hau het SOTA 2024-2025 ket hop it nhat hai nhom decomposition:
•	Wavelet + Transformer: Wavelet cung cap frequency decomposition; Transformer hoc global fusion weight
•	INN + Diffusion: INN dam bao invertibility; Diffusion nang cao chat luong visual
•	Retinex + Deep Learning: Physical prior guide + neural network optimize

9.2. Task-driven Decomposition
Xu huong lon: tich hop downstream task vao loss function cua decomposition. Vi du trong MSRS benchmark, fusion loss duoc joint voi segmentation loss:
L_total = L_fusion + lambda . L_seg + mu . L_det
Dieu nay buoc decomposition phai giu nhung feature quan trong cho detection/segmentation, khong chi toi uu do trung thuc anh.

9.3. Efficient Architecture: Mamba / SSM
Mamba (Gu & Dao, 2023) voi Selective State Space Model (S6) cung cap alternative cho Transformer voi O(n) thay vi O(n^2):
h'(t) = A(t).h(t) + B(t).x(t)
y(t) = C(t).h(t)
A, B, C la time-varying matrices (selective) – khac voi SSM truyen thong fixed. Quan trong cho fusion anh high-resolution (4K medical, satellite imagery).

9.4. Foundation Model va Zero-shot Fusion
Su dung large pre-trained model (SAM, CLIP, Stable Diffusion) lam backbone cho fusion ma khong can fine-tune tren fusion dataset. DDFM (2023) la buoc dau; xu huong 2025 mo rong voi text-guided va multimodal foundation models.

9.5. Interpretable va Medical Fusion
Trong y te, yeu cau interpretability ngay cang cao: FusionINN va cac phuong phap invertible cho phep 'undo' qua trinh fusion, kiem chung xem thong tin tu tung modality dong gop nhu the nao vao ket qua chan doan.
 
10. Ket luan

Image decomposition dong vai tro nen tang trong Image Fusion SOTA. Cac nhan dinh chinh:

•	Khong co phuong phap toi uu toan dien: Multi-scale transform nhanh va interpretable; Deep learning cho chat luong tot hon; Diffusion-based tot nhat ve visual quality nhung cham.
•	Frequency split (low/high) la mau so chung: hau het moi framework deu implicitly hoac explicitly phan tach anh theo chieu nay.
•	INN va invertibility ngay cang quan trong: dac biet trong ung dung y te va cac tinh huong can reconstruction.
•	Diffusion model mo ra paradigm moi: fusion nhu Bayesian inference, khong can fusion-specific training data.
•	Xu huong 2025: Mamba + Wavelet + Task-aware loss la huong co nhieu tiem nang nhat.
